# Cranberry Beans Indian Style
Loosely based off a recipe in the Rancho Gordo cookbook. Tested on the Instapot.

## Ingredients
- 1 c cranberry beans
- 2 tbs coconut oil or ghee
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp fresh ground ginger
- 1/2 tsp cayennr
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 cup of chopped greens, beet greens, turnip greens or chard
- 2 cup water, brought to boiling on stove or electric kettle

## Directions
It works in the Instapot.

1. Click on the sauté button on the Instantpot
2. Heat oil.
3. Add garlic powder, garam masala, cayenne and ginger, sautee for a 3-4 minutes
4. Add 1 cup dried cranberry beans (rinsed)
5. Add 2 cup water, salt, and greens and stir well
6. Press “cancel” to stop sautéing
7. Put on lid, close tight and make sure pressure release is closed
8. Press “pressure cook”, choose high, and set for 25 minutes by using the plus sign
9. Must leave time (extra 10 minutes) to allow to pressure to release on it's own. Ensures beans are cooked but not mushy.
10. Serve with a grain or quinoa.
