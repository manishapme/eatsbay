# Cashew Raisin Rice
Made it up for Instantpot. Flexible, sweet dish. Nuts, tofu and fat make it good as main dish. Could also serve as side to a piece of chicken.

## Ingredients
- 3/4 tsp salt
- 2 tbs ghee
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1/4 tsp clove
- 1/4 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp cayenne
- 1/2 c golden raisins
- 1/2 c cashew pieces
- 1 c brown basmatic rice
- 16 oz tofu, drained and cubed
- 1 c water


## Directions
1. Rinse rice until water runs clear, set aside to drain
2. Set instant pot to saute
3. Heat ghee, when warm
4. Add all ingredients except rice and tofu
5. Saute 4-5 minutes until raisins are plump and you smell the nuts cooking
6. Add tofu and rice, saute 3 more minutes
7. Add water. Close lid and set to cook on "rice" setting
8. Let pressure release naturally, fluff and serve
