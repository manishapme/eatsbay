# Pesto

## Ingredients
- 3 cups basil leaves (or parsley if using walnuts)
- 1/2 c nuts (walnut or pine) lightly toasted
- 4 cloves garlic or 1 tsp granulated
- 1/2 c olive oil
- 1/3 c parmesan
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp lemon juice

## Directions
1. Mix all inhredients in blender
